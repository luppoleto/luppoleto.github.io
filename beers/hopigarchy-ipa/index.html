Hopigarchy IPA

Alas, we must yield to the unrelenting rule of hops. Hop-forward beers catalyzed the craft beer revolution in the good ol’ US of A. Modedration does not live here. Citrusy, piney & resiney hops greet your nose followed by loads of hop flavor. Bready malt & wheat offer temporary repreive before a nice smack of pity bitterness on the finish.
