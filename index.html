Our Story

Luppoleto means hop farm in Italian. It pays homage to the Italian heritage on my mother’s side and agricultural heritage on my father’s side and the craziness I inherited from both sides that led me to plant 60 hop plants nearly ten years ago and set in motion a series of events that led to opening a brewery!
